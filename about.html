About

My name is Chapel, and I'm primarily a writer. I started hacking on Twine a couple years ago and got hooked.

I spend a not insignificant amount of my free time writing code for my own projects and for other Twine authors, to the mild frustration of my family.

I'm also a moderator on the unofficial Twine subreddit and official Twine Discord server.

If you need to contact me, catching up with me on Reddit (/u/ChapelR/) or Discord (@ChapelR#6969) is usually the best way to do that. If it's about my code, open an issue on the appropriate GitHub repository instead.
